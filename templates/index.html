Home Ownership Evolved

Beyond Renting vs. Buying

Common wisdom suggest there are two options to housing: renting or buying. Flock borrows the best of both options to provide a third option. Build equity in a diversified manner without giving up the flexibility of renting.

Diversification

Rule #1 in investing: diversification is the key to long-term success. Why then do most individuals place their all of their eggs in one basket when it comes to their largest investment, their home equity? Flock allows buyers to build home equity in a diversified pool while still living in their home and paying a standard mortgage.

Mobility

One of the largest downsides to homeownership is that it locks buyers into one geographical place. Flock allows its members to move within the system, both intra- and inter-city, without having to go through a burdensome sales process and while continuing to build their equity.

Bulk Buying

This flock flies together. By negotiating as a group of potential buyers rather than each homeowner doing so independently, Flock is able to negotiate better prices on everything from insurance to maintenance costs.
